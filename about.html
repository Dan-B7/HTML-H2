The Catalyst: Ni-Co/MWCNT

The catalyst is a masterpiece of nanotechnology:

  • Nickel (Ni): The workhorse that splits water molecules
  • Cobalt (Co): Protects nickel and boosts efficiency
  • Carbon Nanotubes (MWCNT): Tiny tubes that give a huge surface area for the reaction

UV Light Enhancement

Shining UV light on the catalyst gives it an extra energy boost:

UV Effect: Increases hydrogen production from 150 mL to 250 mL in the same time frame!

The light excites electrons in the catalyst, creating more "reaction hotspots" where hydrogen can form faster.
